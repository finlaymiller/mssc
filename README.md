### Multithreaded Sudoku Solution Checker
Assignment 2 of my Operating Systems course.
===

Provide this with a 9x9 grid of integers as below and it will check whether or not the grid is a valid sudoku solution.
```
1 4 2 3 6 5 7 8 9
6 8 7 1 4 9 2 5 3
5 3 9 7 8 2 6 1 4
4 1 3 6 7 8 5 9 2
8 7 5 2 9 3 1 4 6
2 9 6 4 5 1 3 7 8
9 6 1 8 2 7 4 3 5
7 5 4 9 3 6 8 2 1
3 2 8 5 1 4 9 6 7
```
Build with
`build inst here`
Run with
`run inst here`
